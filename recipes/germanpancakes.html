German Pancakes

Description

A light and fluffy concoction of only 6 ingredients. Made lightning fast and with a rich taste that you won't be able stop eating.

Ingredients

  • 7 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 Tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a blender, add eggs, milk, flour, vanilla, and salt.
  3. Blend until well combined, stopping to scrape flour off sides.
  4. Blend a little more and set aside.
  5. Melt butter in microwave, and pour into a 9x13 glass pan.
  6. Coat all sides and bottom with butter.
  7. Leaving butter in the dish, pour egg mixture into pan.
  8. Bake 30 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and add any preferred pancake toppings.
